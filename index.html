PRODUCTS

Current and Future Products.

Connect

Connect is a socio-commerce app created with the aim to improve online marketing by bridging the gap between the various aspects or players in the distribution chain i.e from producers to final consumer.

Connect creates a medium for easy interaction among these players which makes business to business and business to customer interaction smoother and easier, and easy business to business collaboraion.

Some of the feaures includes direct bargaining, location search filter, advertisement of goods and services, upgrade of account..

This is our first product, and is scheduled to be released in the first half of 2020.




E-edu

E.edu is an e-learning app created to solve the problem of access to standard educational contents in African countries.

E.edu plans to cut across all levels and types of learning that there is and make these contents available without the need to access the internet.

This product is scheduled to be released in the second half of 2020.

SmarGen

SmarGen is a smart electricity provider that uses alternative energy. SmarGen was created to solve the problem of poor electricity supply in Nigerian homes and businesses and by extension, other African homes and businesses.

SmaGen is designed to be a more reliable form of energy than exisitng alternatives and in the long run more economical

Detailed information about this product cannot be made availble to the public yet as it is still at it's early stage of development. This product is scheduled to be released in the second half of 2021.
